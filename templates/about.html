About Syncify

Why did you make this website?
Syncify was created to solve a common problem among Spotify users: combining multiple playlists into one, sorted by the date each song was added. This tool simplifies playlist management and helps users rediscover songs they've added over time across different playlists.

How is my data being used?
Syncify only accesses the Spotify data necessary to combine your playlists. We don't store any of your personal information or playlist data on our servers. The app uses Spotify's official API and adheres to their data usage policies. Your privacy and data security are our top priorities.
